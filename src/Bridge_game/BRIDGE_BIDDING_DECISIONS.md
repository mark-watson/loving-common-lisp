BRIDGE BIDDING DECISIONS

Target Contracts (Combined Points)
•	25+ pts: Game (3NT)
•	26+ pts: Game (4♥ / 4♠)
•	29+ pts: Game (5♣ / 5♦)
•	33+ pts: Small Slam (6-Level)
•	37+ pts: Grand Slam (7-Level)
Special Openings
•	Take out double: opening hand support for unbid suits
•	Preemptive: Potential sacrifice-interference bids
•	Less than opening hand-6 Card Suit bid 2♦ / 2♥ / 2♠
•	with a good (2 honors) 7 Card Suit bid 3♣ / 3♦ / 3♥ / 3♠
1NT Opening
•	Open: 15–17 pts, Balanced hand (no singleton, good doubleton OK)
•	Responses:
•	0–7 pts w/5+ card suit signoff at 2♦ / 2♥ / 2♠; otherwise pass
•	8–9 pts and no 8+ card suit fit bid 2NT (invitational)
•	10–15 pts with 6+ card major signoff at 4♥ / 4♠; or with a 5-card major bid 3♥ / 3♠ (invitational); otherwise 3NT
•	Stayman non-forcing response to 1NT: with 8+ pts and a 4-card major, bid 2♣ to ask opener for a 4-card major (opener with 4 cards in both majors bid 2♥); a 2♦ response by opener denies a 4-card major
Major Openings (1♥ or 1♠)
•	Open: 13–21 pts, 5+ cards. (With both bid the higher-ranking 5-card suit).
•	Responses:
•	0–5 pts: Pass
•	6–10 pts: Raise to 2-Level
•	11–12 pts: Raise to 3-Level
•	13+ pts: Bid to Game
Minor Openings (1♣ or 1♦)
•	Open: 13–21 pts, 3+ cards.
•	Responses:
•	Responder's Priorities: 1. Major suit ➔ 2. Notrump ➔ 3. Minor support
•	Bid a Major (1♥ / 1♠): 6+ pts, 4+ cards. (Tie-breakers: Bid the cheapest 4-card suit, or the higher-ranking 5-card suit).
•	Bid Notrump: * 6–10 pts: 1NT
•	11–12 pts: 2NT
•	13–15 pts: 3NT
•	Raise the Minor (with 4+ card support): * 6–10 pts: Raise to 2-Level
•	11–12 pts: Raise to 3-Level
•	13–15 pts: Bid to Game
Strong Notrump Opening (20-21 pts) 2NT
•	0-4 pts and no 5+ card suit pass; otherwise Stayman applies.
Very Strong Opening 22+ pts, 2♣ (Artificial, Forcing):
•	Responses:
•	< 8 pts: 2♦ (Artificial waiting)
•	8+ pts (Unbalanced): 2♥ / 2♠ / 3♣ / 3♦ (Requires a good 5+ card suit)
•	8+ pts (Balanced): 2NT
Slam Bidding – Asking for Aces and Kings:
•	Blackwood (after suit bids): 4NT; Response: 5♣ / 5♦ / 5♥ / 5♠ / 5NT - 0,1,2,3,4 Aces
•	Blackwood next asking for Kings: 5NT; Response: 6♣ / 6♦ / 6♥ / 6♠ / 6NT - 0,1,2,3,4 Kings
•	Gerber (after NT bids): 4♣; Response: 4♦ / 4♥ / 4♠ / 4NT / 5♣ - 0,1,2,3,4 Aces
•	Gerber next asking for Kings: 5♣; Response: 5♦ / 5♥ / 5♠ / 5NT / 6♣ - 0,1,2,3,4 Kings
